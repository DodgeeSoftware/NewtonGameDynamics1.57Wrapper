Detailed Description

Implement the IPhysicsDebugger to create a physics debugger for your rendering engine. Make call backs for 'pForEachPolygonDo' and 'forEachBodyDo' in your game's main scope and pass them into you own PhysicsDebugger custom made for your rendering engine.
