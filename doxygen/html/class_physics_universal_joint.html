Detailed Description

The PhysicsUniversalJoint is a wrapper around the Newton UniversalJoint PhysicsUniversalJoint implements an IPhysicsJoint and functions to create a sort of double hinge joint where attached bodies may rotate around a pin or around a perpendicular axis to that pin.
